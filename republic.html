Clarion call to the Nation

"Constitution a living document, serving the Nation and strenthing its Citizens


Once termed as a case of "Nero fiddling while Rome was burning" our constitution saw the light of the day under the numinous spark of Independence, gratified by the whole nation in the most ebullient manner; Still carries her head high in the name of "We the People of India"; undoubtedly making of this paragon was a tremendous task and our forefathers toiled their minds out, burning the midnight oil often to make this happen but we can proudly say their handworks are paid off when we see their work is enlivening in our hearts till today.

India i.e. Bharat is today inextricably linked with its soul in a manner never before and in a need never before evidently because of the nature of policies by our iniquitous political power sitting at the throne of Delhi; Enthralled by the the vitality of our holy book in the minds of its aficionados even the Centre is amazed; Though this constitution gave the power to Centre to rule us but at the same limits the power of the Centre and strengthen the hands of "We the people" whenever there is an imminent threat on it, but taking a few steps back we need to understand the underlying problem which is the devotion of a man by the philistine myrmidons which laid the path of degradation and is helping in making his Highness place brazen Politics over the Nation and without a doubt is leading this nation into a jeopardy situation.

Let what the force come by "We the People" should hold fast to Constitutional methods for protecting it and in achieving our social and economic objectives; we need to abandon the bloody methods of revolution; Today the revolution which we need is to be drawn from our holy book and should be imbibed from the spirit of the constitution; As long as Constitutional doors are open we should not open the Grammar of Anarchy; today we need to open our horizons by participating in the social democracy rather than only political democracy; The fundamental blocks such as Equality, Liberty, Fraternity (Brotherhood), Secularism, should be nourished and imbued at every step in our lives

The present government is 'by the people' but in contrast, they are not 'for the people' which is dangerous; Speaking rhetoric vociferously and cudgelling the protestors is a brazen act of their arrogance and accrued power; We need a government which reasons and speak to the people and listen to them out but unfortunately, the present government is nowhere close to this; It is incumbent upon us to answer "How long shall we continue to live in a fool's paradise" The filled in streets across the country is giving the answer "Not any more" and they are not ephemeral in nature instead there are read for a long fight until the government kneels.

Inspiring our spirit from the struggle of freedom fighters and liberal minds of drafting committee today we are determined to defend our Holy book till the last drop of our blood.

'The Constitution is the guide which I never will abandon------George Washington'
Jai Hind, Jai Constitution
