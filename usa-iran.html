Who was General Qassem Soleimani?

He was commander of Quds Force, the external operations wing of Iran’s elite Islamic Revolutionary Group(IRGC); for a long time Soleimani was in charge of foreign operations, which included building networks and proxies across west Asia; He was highly revered in Iran and outside in pro-Iran regions, he became an iconic figure when he lead the war against ISIS through his network of militias whose help even the US took to fight ISIS; he gained so much popularity that he became a national figure above the politics
